Add the `ArcCluster` kit for recreating a Reuters.com homepage "cluster" inside Arc's graphics container module. Drop a graphic into `ArcCluster`'s `header`, `stage` and `footer` snippets and use `ArcHeader` for a homepage-style title block. `ArcCluster` loads the version of the Reuters Knowledge font that Reuters.com uses automatically; `ArcFonts` is also exported for loading that font on its own.
