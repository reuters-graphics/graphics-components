Add the `ArcEmbed` kit for building iframed embeds that match the Reuters.com homepage. Drop a graphic into `ArcEmbed`'s `header`, `stage` and `footer` snippets and use `ArcHeader` for a homepage-style title block. `ArcEmbed` loads the version of the Reuters Knowledge font that Reuters.com uses automatically; `ArcFonts` is also exported for loading that font outside an `ArcEmbed`.
