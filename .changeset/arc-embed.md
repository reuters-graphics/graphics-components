Add the `ArcEmbed` kit (`ArcEmbed`, `ArcHeader`, `ArcFonts`) for building iframed embeds that match the Reuters.com homepage. Drop a graphic into `ArcEmbed`'s `header`, `stage` and `footer` snippets, use `ArcHeader` for a homepage-style title block, and render `ArcFonts` once to load the Reuters Knowledge web font.
